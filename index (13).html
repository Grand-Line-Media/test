Our Story

Where Every Bite Feels Like Home

Taqueria Calvillo was born from a simple belief: that great Mexican food doesn't need shortcuts. Rooted in tradition and seasoned with family pride, we bring the authentic flavors of Mexico to Montrose, Colorado — one taco at a time.

From our hand-pressed corn tortillas to our slow-cooked meats and house-made salsas, everything on our menu is prepared fresh daily. Whether you're a local or just passing through on your way to Telluride, you'll leave with a full belly and a reason to come back.
